About Us

Surabaya Dev merupakan Kumpulan orang yang suka dengan Update Teknologi, Belajar, Berbagi dan suka Berkontribusi. Surabaya Dev terbagi dalam 6 Divisi.

Our Division

Surabaya Dev terdiri dari beberapa divisi yang selalu berusaha untuk
menghasilkan karya

  • Social Media & Content Creative

  • Graphic Designer

  • Podcaster Host Streamer

  • Dev Team

  • Management Event Community

  • Admin & Logistik
Social media & content creative is a person who is responsible for
managing social media and creating creative content concepts
Graphic designer is person who have responsible in making visualization
an information with computer software.
Podcaster host streamer is a person who is responsible for intermediary
interview communication and as a host on events/live/podcasts
Dev team is the person responsible for creating the Surabaya Dev website
project and contributing to making information about programming
Management event community is the person responsible
for organizing and planning the event
Admin & Logistik is the person responsible for managing
the administration and distribution of goods

Event

Surabaya Dev memiliki 3 macam kegiatan untuk meningkatkan skill tech
di bidang programming maupun digital teknologi

Meetup

(Seminar/Webinar) Kegiatan bersama topik teknologi & programming secara online maupun offline

Upgradding Skill

(Workshop) Pelatihan internal pengurus berupa workshop untuk pengembangan diri seputar teknikal programming

Kolaborasi Komunitas

Sinergi antar komunitas guna untuk mendukung kegiatan komunitas lokal teknologi di Indonesia
